Coconut Rice

Another very popular dish in the Colombian's Coast Gastronomy.

    Ingredients

  • 3 cups Rice
  • 500 ml Coconut milk
  • 5 tablespoons Sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons Salt
  • Raisins to taste
  • Water as needed

    Steps

  1. Measure out 1 cup (approximately 250ml) of pure coconut milk and set it aside. Mix the remaining 250ml of coconut milk with enough water to make 6 cups (approximately 1.5 liters) of coconut milk mixture.
  2. In a deep pot, combine the 1 cup of pure coconut milk, sugar, and salt. Heat the mixture over medium heat, stirring constantly, until it thickens and starts to separate into oil and a brown, coconut-flake-like mixture (titote).
  3. Continue stirring the titote until it turns a deep brown color. Be careful not to burn the mixture.
  4. Immediately pour in the 6 cups of coconut milk mixture and bring to a simmer.
  5. Add the 3 cups of rice and stir constantly until bubbles start to form on the surface.
  6. Reduce the heat to low and cover the pot with a tight-fitting lid. Cook for 10 minutes.
  7. Add raisins to taste, stir gently, and continue cooking for another 5 minutes with the lid on.
  8. Turn off the heat and let the rice rest for a minute or two before serving. Your coconut rice with raisins is now ready!
